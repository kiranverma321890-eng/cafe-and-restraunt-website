Our Story

A Cozy Escape in the Heart of Indira Nagar

Welcome to Sky Face Cafe & Restaurant, a peaceful oasis designed for great conversations and delicious food. Whether you are looking for a quiet spot for a date, a place to celebrate a birthday, or just crave mouth-watering vegetarian cuisine, we have created the perfect ambiance for you.

Rated 4.6 by nearly 500 happy guests, we take pride in our warm service, private seating options, and a menu that blends comforting classics with modern cafe favorites. From crispy Spring Rolls to creamy Shakes, every item is crafted with love.
